about us

who we are

eabag studios is a digital agency specialising in brand design, web development and 3D animation

At Teabag, we believe the devil is in the details. From the first phone call to the final product, we know it’s the small things that make the big things truly outstanding. We realize, in fact, that size really does matter. That’s why Teabag remains a tightly knit and fiercely independent creative hub where designers freely share thoughts and inspire ideas, rather than a huge, anonymous agency where even the PA has a PA and your project is just another job on a list. Instead, at Teabag, clients deal directly with our designers on every aspect of their project: no question is too trivial, no problem too great and we are always willing to go that extra mile to meet your needs.

about Art deco 20

An award-winning creative media company specializing in brand design, web development and 3d animation, Teabag strives to challenge the way everyone – maybe even you - thinks about your brand, product or website. Our innovative, forward thinking and hugely experienced design team pride themselves on being able to get into your head and take your vision to the next level. As far as we’re concerned, we’re in it together.
